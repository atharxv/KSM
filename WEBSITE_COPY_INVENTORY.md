Body: "Some people walk into a room and others adjust. KSM is a symbol of status for a generation that moves fast and understands that status is visible, a cultural reference, the name people read as ambition and taste before anyone speaks. KSM is for those who have already arrived."

### Timeline
Eyebrow: THE PROCESS
Headline: From Vision to Vessel

#### 01 Conception
Every piece starts as a conviction. We decide what the garment needs to say before we decide what it needs to look like. Silhouette, weight, presence — resolved before a single pattern is cut.

#### 02 Sourcing
Cotton sourced for how it holds. Italy chosen for what it knows. Each fabric answers one question: will it still look right in three years.

#### 03 Construction
Cut and assembled in an atelier in Piedmont. Reinforced collar tape builds structure that holds its shape over time. Nothing here is accidental.

#### 04 Refinement
A soft-finish print process that sits inside the fabric, not on top of it. The result doesn't crack. It doesn't peel. It reads the same on day one as it does on day three hundred.
